The quality or state of being objective. In philosophy, objectivity is the concept of [[20220415254500]] truth independent from individual subjectivity. A proposition is considered to have objective truth when its truth conditions are met without bias caused by a sentient subject. Scientific objectivity refers to the ability to judge without external influence. (*Wikipedia*)
